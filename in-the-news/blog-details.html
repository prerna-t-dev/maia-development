Everything You Need to Know About Luxury Apartments and Upcoming Projects in Bangalore

Mayela Lozano

June 12, 2025
5 min. read

Summary Title

Bangalore, often hailed as the Silicon Valley of India, has seen a massive surge in demand for residential spaces especially luxury apartments that cater to the refined tastes of urban dwellers. Whether you’re choosing between an apartment and an independent house or exploring upcoming residential projects, this guide, curated from real discussions and insights will help you make an informed decision.

1. What's Better: Living in an Apartment or an Independent House in Bangalore?

Both types of accommodations have their own unique advantages. The better choice depends on the factors that matter most to you. Here’s a breakdown of key differences:

Apartments

  • Top builders in Bangalore, such as MAIA Estates’ residences are built to global standards, featuring top-of-the-line materials and intelligent layouts
  • Built-in amenities such as 24/7 security, parking, and power backup come as standard. MAIA Estates’ upcoming apartments in Bangalore offer ultra-luxury features.
  • Easier loan approvals from banks.
  • Access to an in-house maintenance team.
  • Strong potential for capital appreciation.
  • Encourages community living and social harmony.

Independent Houses

  • Higher upfront investment required.
  • Additional efforts and costs needed for basic amenities.
  • Banks perform stricter loan scrutiny.
  • Maintenance is time-consuming and recurring.
  • Comparatively lower capital appreciation.
  • Offers greater privacy.
  • Risk of scams for first-time or inexperienced buyers.

2. How Do I Find Residential Apartments in Bangalore?

There are several reliable ways to find the best residential apartments in Bangalore:

1. Online Portals: Websites like 99acres and NoBroker can be helpful, but it’s always safer to visit the official websites of reputed real estate builders in Bangalore. For instance, if you’re searching for premium apartments in areas like Richmond Road or Jakkur, connect directly with MAIA Estates via their official website
2. Local Brokers: A real estate agent can help you find homes in specific localities.
3. Social Media & WhatsApp Groups: These platforms often have leads on direct-owner listings.
4. Local Search: Walk or drive around preferred localities to spot “To-Let” signs or inquire with security personnel in apartment buildings.

3. What Are the Key Elements of Luxury Home Interiors in Bangalore?

Luxury apartments in Bangalore, especially those developed by top developers like MAIA Estates, reflect a shift in design sensibilities. Key elements include:

  • materials that are carefully selected not only for their aesthetic appeal but also for their lasting strength and functionality
  • Smart home technology for security and utility automation.
  • Intelligent lighting systems that adjust to your mood.
  • Custom-built furniture ideal for work-from-home setups.
  • Modular kitchen and bathroom designs.
  • Spacious, well-ventilated layouts that integrate nature with living.

If you’re looking for the pinnacle of modern luxury, take Pelican Grove by MAIA Estates as your benchmark. Rising 19 floors with just 36 exclusive apartments, it offers unmatched privacy, private clubs and panoramic views of Jakkur Lake.

Prefer something closer to the heart of the city? Explore 27 Summit at Richmond Road—MAIA’s signature offering in Central Bangalore.

4. Which Are the New and Upcoming Projects in Bangalore?

Bangalore is booming with luxury real estate, and top apartment builders like MAIA Estates are leading this transformation.

A few noteworthy examples:

  • Pelican Grove, Jakkur Lake: A serene location with 19 floors, 36 limited-edition apartments, private Jacuzzis, and clubs at ground and sky levels. The project was completed in just 3 years, a testament to MAIA Estates’ credibility and efficiency.
  • 27 Summit, Richmond Road: Spanning 2.6 acres, this project features three exclusive wings, three penthouses, and three private clubs. Sustainability is a core pillar, making it one of the most eco-conscious premium residential projects in Bangalore.

5. What Are Some Upcoming Residential Projects in Bangalore and How to Choose the Right One?

Choosing the right residential project in a city like Bangalore can feel overwhelming. Here’s a guide:

Research the Builder: Look for reputed builders in Bangalore like MAIA Estates. Check their completed projects and visit ongoing ones to get a feel for their quality.
Understand Your Needs: Match what the builder is offering with your expectations, especially in terms of layout, amenities, and pricing.
Location Matters: Ensure the project is well-connected to your workplace, healthcare facilities, recreational zones, and major city arteries.

For example, 27 Summit by MAIA Estates on Richmond Road is gaining traction among HNIs for good reason—it offers 40+ world-class amenities, private concierge services, sustainable architecture, and top-tier finishes.
